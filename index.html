Our Vision

There are over 31 lakh NGOs in our country and thousands of noble souls who feel for the needy and wish to make a difference. For us, this means millions of opportunities to help. In this fast paced world, where time is that biggest constraint, we cant to make the process of helping the needy extremely effective and efficient by being the catalyst. We have noticed that people help the NGOs they are aware of in whatever way possible (by giving good/services). However, the main probless that occurs is that these NGOs may not need the goods being given to them. We feel that no noble deed should ever go to waste. Thus we connect the donor to the NGO who requires the goods. Thus increasing the effect and efficiency of every noble deed being carried out. Our main goal is to bridge the gap between the 'haves' and 'have-nots' by being a "new age robin hood". We are here to make every noble deed effective.

Every helping hand will help beautify India

Our mission is to help you donate household items to any cause so that your help reaches the needy. GivingThanks is a donation platform that allows you to donate various items to verified NGOs.

NGOs

The Government of India has approved the NGOs who are registered with us. The NGO can directly log-in and get in touch with the donors to ask them details about the products.

GivingThanks

We provide a platform to connect the donors who have the goods to donate to the NGOs who require them. We believe that no noble deed must go to waste and thus we make the process as efficient as possible.

Donors

Donors can list the items which they would like on our website, can provide details for NGOs to contact them. They can then set up a convenient pick up time for the goods to be picked up.
